Registering Machines


To register a new machine click the 'Machines' button or use the navigation bar.

Then enter the machines description and click the 'Create Machine button' on the machines page.

The newly created machine and the generated authentication token are then listed on the page.

Inspecting Parts


To inspect parts click the 'Parts' button or use the navigation bar.

Finished parts and their respective key attributes are listed on the parts page.

To save the generated sensor data of a part click the download link and to delete the part use the deletion link.
